About Us

We are students from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila with a goal of preventing the rise of weather-related illness. Climate change has affected our country's climate; we should learn to combat this impact on us.

Our Goals

Climate Action: Taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts by raising awareness and building resilience in our community.

Good Health and Well-being: Our goal is to promote good health and well-being by helping people be aware of illnesses in their community and how to stay safe.

Our Direction

Vision

To envision a resilient community in Manila where every citizen is equipped with the knowledge and tools to proactively protect themselves against weather-triggered diseases, ensuring a healthier future despite a changing climate.

Mission

To provide accessible, real-time information and educational resources regarding weather-related health risks. We strive to bridge the gap between data and public safety, empowering PLM students and locals to make informed health decisions.
