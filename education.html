Weather & Diseases Education Page

Learn how different weather conditions can affect your health, and how to stay safe.

üî• Hot Weather (Around 30¬∞C - 40¬∞C)

1. Heat Stroke

Symptoms: High fever, confusion, dizziness, rapid heartbeat.

Tips: Stay hydrated, avoid staying under direct sunlight for long, wear light clothes.

First Aid: Move the person to a cooler place, apply cold compress, give water if conscious, seek medical help immediately.

2. Dehydration

Symptoms: Dry mouth, headache, dark urine, dizziness.

Tips: Drink plenty of water, avoid too much physical activity outdoors during peak heat.

First Aid: Let the person rest, give water or electrolyte drink.

3. Skin Rashes / Prickly Heat

Symptoms: Red itchy bumps, stinging sensation.

Tips: Take cool showers, wear loose clothing.

First Aid: Apply cool compress and soothing lotion.

üåß Rainy Weather (Around 24¬∞C - 30¬∞C)

1. Dengue Fever

Symptoms: High fever, severe headache, joint pain, rashes.

Tips: Avoid stagnant water, use mosquito repellents.

First Aid: Give fluids, rest, and seek medical attention.

2. Leptospirosis

Symptoms: Fever, muscle pain, headache, yellow skin.

Tips: Avoid walking in flood water, wear boots if necessary.

First Aid: Wash exposed skin with soap, seek medical treatment quickly.

3. Colds & Flu

Symptoms: Runny nose, fever, cough, body aches.

Tips: Strengthen your immune system, avoid getting soaked in the rain.

First Aid: Rest, warm fluids, paracetamol for fever.

‚ùÑ Cold Weather (Around 18¬∞C - 24¬∞C)

1. Hypothermia

Symptoms: Shivering, slurred speech, weak pulse.

Tips: Wear warm clothes, avoid staying outside too long.

First Aid: Move to a warm place, wrap in blankets, give warm drinks.

2. Asthma Attacks

Symptoms: Difficulty breathing, coughing, wheezing.

Tips: Avoid cold air exposure, wear masks or scarves.

First Aid: Use inhaler and stay calm.

3. Pneumonia

Symptoms: Cough with phlegm, fever, chills, chest pain.

Tips: Keep warm, stay dry, boost immune system.

First Aid: Seek medical care; rest and fluids.

üíß Humid Weather (Above 70% Humidity)

1. Fungal Skin Infections

Symptoms: Itchy patches, redness, peeling skin.

Tips: Keep skin dry, avoid tight clothing.

First Aid: Use antifungal creams.

2. Heat Exhaustion

Symptoms: Heavy sweating, dizziness, nausea.

Tips: Stay hydrated, avoid physical exertion.

First Aid: Move to a cool place, drink water.

3. Respiratory Problems

Symptoms: Difficulty breathing, cough, wheezing.

Tips: Keep indoor air clean, avoid pollution.

First Aid: Rest, inhale steam, consult a doctor.
